Nature's Elements Meet Their Master

Transporting and distributing liquids like calcium chloride brine, salt brine and other premium branded liquids during winter months should be treated with care. With ICE MASTER SYSTEMS® by Enduraplas, you can become the master of winter weather with quality liquid ice control equipment. Be a Master™.

Why Ice Master Systems®

We Know Liquids

Since the birth of our company, we have taken pride in manufacturing simple solutions that help our customers manage their liquid better. Our vision is to eliminate ice-related accidents. When it comes to liquids, we focus on three key areas; storing, transporting and applying. Our storage and transport solutions include a large range of poly tanks that offer unique durability allowing our customers to manage expensive liquids with confidence. Our liquid application equipment includes a large range of spray equipment. Our anti-icing and de-icing, liquid salt brine sprayers will also give you a competitive edge before and after the storm.

We Back What We Build

Adding a durable, long-lasting piece of equipment to your fleet means a lot to us. The industry-leading warranties we offer are our guarantee that you’re investing in a product that is built for endurance. We know that unneeded product failures and breakdowns can cost a lot of money. We are hard at work every day testing different solutions that overcome this real-life problem. That’s why every piece of equipment is developed with two core principles in mind. Make it simple-to use. Make it last longer than the industry average.

We Listen To Professionals

Every unit we design is customer-inspired. How is this achieved? Our engineers are spending countless hours working with professionals who everyday depend on ice control equipment to get their job done quickly. This involves testing, trialing and re-designing until the best possible solution arises. Manufacturing a superior product requires a lot of research, creativity, dedication and a strong commitment to the customer that will be depending on it.

We Believe In Simplicity

Simply put, every problem can be overcome with a simple solution. Our products are developed so minimal training is required to get the equipment up-and-running before the storm arrives. Less complication means less chance of failure.

We Put Safety First

We recognize that manufacturing salt brine equipment that help prevent injuries on sidewalks and save lives on roads is a big responsibility – and we’ve accepted that responsibility. While our products are designed with the operator at top-of-mind, we believe the result is what matters most. This means that every inch of ground where liquid salt brine is applied, safety is the highest priority.

We're Not Stopping Here

With a dedicated team of engineers and research specialists, we are truly setting a new standard for liquids in the snow and ice industry. We have several new products on the horizon that will transform the way governments, municipalities and independent landscape professionals use liquids. These products include; The Liquid Master™ W-Series: a walk-behind liquid ice control sprayer, and the Brine Master™: an easy-to-operate brine maker – just to mention a couple.
