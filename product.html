Liquid Master® EM-Series

The Liquid Master® EM-Series is an industry-first high-flow 12V brine sprayer. This anti-icing and de-icing spray system is ideal for quietly and efficiently spraying liquid brine on roads, around parking lots and building entries using the electric pump, while still being able to utilize GPS flow-control. Each salt brine unit features a stainless steel frame and baffle balls that stop liquid surge. *Stand or legs not included – Sold separately.

Product Code: CONFIGLMEM
